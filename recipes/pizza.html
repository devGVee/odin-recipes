Pizza

Description

This is a simple and delicious homemade pizza recipe that you can customize with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 12-inch pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  4. Add sliced pepperoni, mushrooms, and onions to the pizza.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
